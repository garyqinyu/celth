PegFont
 

The PegFont structure contains format and data address information for PEG compatible fonts. 

The PegFont structure is defined as: 

struct PegFont
{
    UCHAR uType;
    UCHAR uAscent;
    UCHAR uDescent;
    UCHAR uHeight;
    WORD  wBytesPerLine;
    WORD  wFirstChar;
    WORD  wLastChar;
    WORD  *pOffsets;
    UCHAR *pWidths;
    UCHAR *pData;
};
