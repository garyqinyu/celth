PEG Support Page

The developers at Swell Software, Inc. are committed to making PEG the best embedded GUI solution available at any price. We are also firmly committed to making your project development effort a success. We value your feedback, input, comments, and criticisms as these help to insure that the PEG library, utility programs, and supporting documentation continue to grow and improve to meet your needs.

Unlimited technical support is provided to licensed users of PEG for the term of the support contract. The normal support contract extends for 6 months from the date of purchase.

If you purchased PEG from one of our integrated resellers, your first line of support is the reseller from whom you purchased PEG. General questions regarding build procedures, debugging, compiler settings, run-time libraries etc. should be directed to your reseller whenever possible.

For technical questions of a non-urgent nature, email support at Swell Software is the best solution. We encourage you to provide a detailed description of the problem you are reporting or question you have. If possible it is also beneficial to include the related application level code that is not working as you expect. In most cases we will duplicate your problem before generating a fix to insure that the problem is corrected. E-mail support requests are always answered within 4 work-hours of receipt.

Send your E-mail support requests to:  support@swellsoftware.com
